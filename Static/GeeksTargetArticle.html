"Indo-Pakistani War of 1947"
The Indo-Pakistani War of 1947-1948, sometimes known as the First Kashmir War, was fought between India and Pakistan over the princely state of Kashmir and Jammu. It was the first of four Indo-Pakistan Wars fought between the two newly independent nations.
