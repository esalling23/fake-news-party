Wait Wait Don't Tell Meme

Based on the popular NPR game show Wait, Wait Don't Tell Me, Wait, Wait Don't Tell MEME is a investigative bluffing game that teaches players how to quickly fact check image based viral content. This game puts students in the role of conversation-shapers by tasking them to create memes about cultural and political issues through playful acts of creation. They will then learn to parse content for media messages, intent, and impact by ranking the created memes by various measures including political spectrum, tone, intention, and "truthfulness."

Wiki Geeks

WikiGeeks is a realtime race played on Wikipedia.org that pits players against each other in a search for specific pages using only links embedded within articles. This game tests a broad cross section of knowledge, especially knowing how different topics relate to one another and how they are mapped digitally. It has the secondary benefit of helping students become familiar with Wikipedia's pages and navigation.
